The definition of gamification

Gamification (from the English word 'gamification') is an approach aimed at increasing the motivation, active participation, and effectiveness of participants by applying game-like elements and mechanics in non-game contexts, including the field of education.This concept became widespread at the beginning of the 21st century with the rapid development of digital technologies and has established itself in areas such as learning, business, healthcare, and public services.
